It's a BeaUtIfuL DaY!

The germ of the idea behind WaT's tHe DaY 2dAy stems from two sources. Firstly, from the yearly wall calendar that I happened to receive from the Baptist Hospital Bangalore where my wife delivered our first child in the year 2017. The calendar had almost all the important days in the field of medical science highlighted. I thought how wonderful it would be to have a web app or a mobile app which reminds us eary morning that today 25th of September is the World Pharmacist Day. So why not wish the local pharmacist A Happy Pharmacist Day for the wonderful job he is doing. Secondly, from the pretty popular initiative of Google Doodle's which enlightens the world everyday about the sung and unsung heroes/events from the present and past. How about helping the Leprosy Mission International in spreading the message that Leprosy is curable on the 31st of January every year. Or joining the WHO in making people aware that it's absolutely Ok to have been through or going through depression on the 10th of October every year.

We are the ones who are trying to channel the pandemonium of our inspirations and our frustrations into something more meaningful. Meaningful enough so that the TODAY in our lives is better than the YESTERDAY. And the TOMORROW even better.
